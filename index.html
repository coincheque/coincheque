Roadmap

The journey from an idea to meme crypto cheques on multiple blockchains

01

Project Launch

The concept emerged in late 2023 to develop a meme NFT collection resembling bank cheques, with denominations in each chain's native coins. It debuted on Ethereum and expanded to other major blockchains in 2024. The latest series is the Bitcoin Ordinals edition.

The first Coincheque
02

Contracts and uniqueness

The Ethereum collection uses OpenSea smart-contracts, making it compatible with leading marketplaces. The Solana edition was built with Crossmint, a prominent enterprise blockchain platform. The collections on other EVM chains use Opensea and NFTrade multi-chain marketplace's smart-contracts. Each Coincheque NFT and each denomination is completely unique.
